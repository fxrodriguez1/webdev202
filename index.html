Bus Tracker

The Bus Tracker was made to help people near Lane Tech find out when the next bus will come at either Western or Addison Stops. It uses the CTA Bus Traker API to find predicted times of arrival for the buses. It is broken up into different pages for the separate streets, after a hme page with links to each route based on which one you are accessing.

Find Affordable Rental Housing

This project uses data from the City of Chicago data portal to display affordable rental housing within the city limits of Chicago inside a radius set by the user, on top of a google map with markers distinguishing where the affordable housing is, along with other important information.
